Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: October 2026 • LifeLink Command Center

1. Privacy Policy & Data Security

1.1 Information We Collect

When you register for the LifeLink Network, we collect necessary personal and medical data, including but not limited to: your full name, location, contact information, blood type, medical history (for eligibility), and organ pledge status. This data is strictly used for the purpose of matching donors with recipients.

1.2 Medical Data Encryption

We treat your medical data with the highest level of security. All biological phenotype data, blood groups, and eligibility records are encrypted at rest and in transit. We adhere strictly to national health data protection standards to ensure your privacy is never compromised.

1.3 Third-Party Disclosure

LifeLink does not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your information is only shared with verified, registered healthcare providers, hospitals, and emergency response teams (such as 104/108 operators) strictly during active donation matches or emergencies.

2. Terms of Service

2.1 Eligibility and Honesty

By using LifeLink, you agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information regarding your health and eligibility. Providing false medical information poses a severe risk to potential recipients. Users found submitting fraudulent health data will be permanently banned and reported to relevant medical authorities.

2.2 Medical Disclaimer

LifeLink is a coordination and routing platform, not a healthcare provider. The "Smart Eligibility Checker" provides estimates based on standard parameters but does not replace professional medical advice. Final eligibility for blood or organ donation is determined exclusively by certified medical personnel at the time of donation.

2.3 Non-Commercial Use

LifeLink is strictly a voluntary, non-profit coordination network. Any attempt to solicit compensation, payment, or financial reward in exchange for blood or organ donation through this platform is strictly prohibited and illegal under the Transplantation of Human Organs Act.

By continuing to use the LifeLink platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by these Privacy Policies and Terms of Service.
