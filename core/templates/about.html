Blood Donation

The Science Behind It

Donating just one unit of blood can save up to three lives. The blood is separated into red cells, plasma, and platelets, supporting community health and emergency readiness globally.

Organ Pledge

Myths vs. Facts

A common myth is that medical conditions prevent donation. In reality, very few conditions automatically disqualify donors. Specialized medical teams evaluate viability at the time of donation.

Emergency Grid

Platelets & Transfusion

Platelets have a very short shelf life and are crucial for supporting critical care patients, such as those undergoing cancer treatments. Timely and consistent donor availability is essential.
